About Me

Umme Ummara is a passionate PhD researcher in the field of Innovation and Management of Public Resources at the University of Molise. With a focus on People Engagement in Direct Democracy, her research delves into the comparative analysis of Italian referendums, drawing insights from the UK and Canada. Ummara's research holds the potential to shape future approaches to public engagement in democratic societies.
